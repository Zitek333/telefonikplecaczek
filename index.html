Search Tips & Tricks

Use Quotes for Exact Phrases

Example: "how to use Google" finds that exact phrase

Exclude Words with Minus

Example: cars -electric shows cars but not electric ones

Search Within a Site

Example: site:wikipedia.org artificial intelligence

Find Similar Sites

Example: related:amazon.com finds sites like Amazon

üë§ Creating a Google Account

A Google Account gives you access to all Google services with one login!

Steps to Create Your Account

1 Go to Google Account Creation

Visit accounts.google.com and click "Create account"

2 Fill in Your Information
  • First and last name
  • Choose a username (this becomes your Gmail address)
  • Create a strong password
  • Confirm your password
3 Add Recovery Information

Phone number and recovery email (optional but recommended)

4 Verify Your Account

Google may send a verification code to your phone

üîê Security Tip: Enable 2-factor authentication for extra security!
